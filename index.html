FAQ

Hair Extension Questions, Answered

Every question deserves a thoughtful answer. If yours isn't here, your consultation is the place to ask.

Send Your Hair Photos for Review
How much do hair extensions cost in Burbank / Los Angeles?
Pricing depends on the method, hair length, grams, hair quality and the desired result. Targeted volume enhancements can start from $400, while full length transformations typically begin from $1,000+. A private consultation is required for an accurate quote tailored to your specific needs.
What hair extension method is best for me?
The best method depends on your natural hair density, scalp sensitivity, lifestyle, desired length and maintenance habits. During consultation, Lana evaluates your hair and recommends the most suitable method — whether K-Tip, micro-capsule, BIO Tape or a hybrid approach.
Are K-Tip and micro-capsule extensions damaging?
When installed correctly and maintained properly, these methods can be comfortable and natural-looking without damaging your natural hair. The health of the natural hair always comes first, which is why consultation and correct maintenance are a non-negotiable part of the process at Culture of Extensions.
How long do hair extensions last?
Longevity depends on the method, hair quality, aftercare routine and maintenance schedule. Many clients need a maintenance move-up every 8–10 weeks, but the exact timeline is personalized based on your natural hair growth rate and lifestyle.
Do I need a consultation before booking?
Yes, always. Consultation allows Lana to evaluate your current hair, desired result, color match, density goals, previous extension history and budget before creating a personalized plan. This protects your hair health and ensures you receive the right service at the right investment.
Do you offer professional hair extension education?
Yes. Culture Academy by Lana offers professional hair extension education for stylists who want to learn consultation, placement logic, blending, maintenance, correction and luxury client experience. Apply for training through the Academy section below.
Where is Culture of Extensions located?
Culture of Extensions serves clients in Burbank, Glendale, Los Angeles, Toluca Lake, Studio City, Pasadena and surrounding areas by private appointment. The studio is located at 2119 N Glenoaks Blvd, Burbank, CA.
